## What is OPCAmerica?

OPCAmerica is the first institution in the American continent dedicated exclusively to OPC (OPC UA and OPC Classic) education, resources, AI tools, and consulting — in both English and Spanish.

We serve industrial automation professionals from Canada to Argentina: engineers, system integrators, plant managers, and developers who work with OPC protocols and need quality resources in their language.

## The 4 Pillars

| Pillar | What it is |
|---|---|

| 👥 **Community** | Bilingual forum, success cases, professional network |
| 🤖 **AI Tools** | OPC AI Assistant, node diagnostics, config generator |

## Key Decisions Already Made

- ✅ **Domain:** opcamerica.com — secured (.com chosen for max trust + SEO)
- ✅ **Language strategy:** Bilingual EN + ES from day one, English-first at launch
- ✅ **URL structure:** `/en/` and `/es/` subdirectories
- ✅ **OPC terms in Spanish:** Always keep in English (namespace, node, subscription, etc.)
- ✅ **Pricing:** USD for North America, PPP-adjusted (~50%) for Latam
- ✅ **Business model:** 4 streams — courses, marketplace/skills, AI SaaS, consulting
- ✅ **Recommended stack:** Next.js + Sanity CMS + Stripe + Claude API + Vercel
