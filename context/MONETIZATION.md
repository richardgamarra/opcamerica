# OPCAmerica — Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Architecture Overview

OPCAmerica operates 4 revenue streams designed to work together: free content builds audience → courses and membership convert audience → AI tools create recurring SaaS revenue → consulting captures enterprise value.

Each stream is bilingual. English content targets higher ARPU (North America). Spanish content targets higher volume (Latin America).

## Pricing Philosophy

### North America (USA, Canada)
- Full USD pricing as listed above
- Enterprise clients budget for $5,000–25,000 projects easily
- Focus messaging on ROI, compliance, and integration cost reduction

### Latin America
- Latam pricing = 40–60% of USD price (PPP-adjusted)
- Display in local currency where possible (MXN, COP, ARS, CLP, PEN)
- Offer local payment methods (OXXO in Mexico, PSE in Colombia, etc.)
- Annual plans are more attractive in Latam than monthly (currency stability)

### Freemium Strategy
- Free tier: 10 tutorials, read-only forum, newsletter
- This free tier exists purely as a funnel to paid products
- Free users should experience real value before hitting a paywall
- Never put core SEO content behind a paywall — it is the acquisition engine
