What does OPC mean?
OPC stands for
One-Person
Company
A One-Person Company is a real, scalable business operated by a single founder using AI tools, automation, and software — instead of a large team. It's not freelancing. It's not a side hustle. It's a full business built for leverage.
OPCAmerica is the first bilingual community and resource platform dedicated exclusively to OPC founders across the Americas — connecting, equipping, and accelerating solo builders from Canada to Argentina.
